History about PT-141

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) was developed from Melanotan II as a targeted melanocortin receptor agonist. Initial research in the early 2000s discovered its potential for treating sexual dysfunction, particularly hypoactive sexual desire disorder. Unlike traditional erectile dysfunction medications, PT-141 works through melanocortin pathways in the central nervous system to enhance sexual desire and arousal in both men and women. It received FDA approval in 2019 for treating acquired, generalized hypoactive sexual desire disorder in premenopausal women.
