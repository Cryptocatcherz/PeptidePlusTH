Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: Due to the sensitive nature of research chemicals and strict quality control requirements, we have specific return and refund policies outlined below.

1. General Return Policy

All sales of research peptides are considered final due to:

  • The sensitive nature of research chemicals
  • Product integrity and quality control requirements
  • Regulatory compliance and safety standards
  • Inability to verify proper storage and handling once shipped

2. Eligible Returns

We will accept returns ONLY in the following circumstances:

2.1 Manufacturing Defects

  • Visibly damaged or contaminated product upon arrival
  • Incorrect product shipped (different peptide than ordered)
  • Incorrect quantity received
  • Product with broken seals or compromised packaging before delivery

2.2 Shipping Errors

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Missing items from order
  • Damaged during transit (with carrier documentation)

3. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are NOT eligible for return or refund:

  • Opened or unsealed products
  • Products stored improperly after delivery
  • Products past the claim period (see below)
  • Special orders or custom preparations
  • Customer buyer's remorse or change of mind
  • Products used for any purpose

4. Return Process

Step-by-Step Return Instructions

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Email returns@peptideplaza.com within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide Order Details: Include order number, product name, and issue description
  3. Photo Evidence: Attach clear photos showing:
    • Unopened product with intact seals
    • Damage or defect
    • Original packaging
    • Shipping label
  4. Await Authorization: Do NOT ship items back without return authorization (RA) number
  5. Ship Back: Once approved, ship product back in original packaging with RA number clearly marked

5. Refund Process

5.1 Refund Eligibility

If your return is approved, you may be eligible for:

  • Full Refund: For unopened, defective products
  • Store Credit: For exchange of equal or greater value
  • Replacement: Same product sent at no additional cost

5.2 Refund Method

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method when possible
  • For cryptocurrency payments, refunds may be issued in the same cryptocurrency
  • Refund amounts are calculated at the exchange rate at time of refund

5.3 Refund Timeline

  • Approval: Within 3-5 business days of receiving returned product
  • Processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Total Time: 10-15 business days from receipt of return
